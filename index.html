Challenges I Faced

Some challenges I faced were particularily with edge detection. When doing the homework I could not find the time to implement the segment detection. My implementation just checked what line was the closest, and those extend past the shape. Another challenge I faced was calculating the scaling factor. Originally I calculated it based on distance to center, but when you scaled it smaller and went past the center it scaled larger again. Another issue I had is that when the shape gets really small its hard to scale it larger again.
